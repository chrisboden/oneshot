Grounding

In the context of AI, grounding means connecting the model's responses to reliable sources of information, such as provided documents, files, or real-time web search results. This prevents hallucination and ensures the output is factually accurate and relevant to the specific task.

Key Takeaways

  • New idea → Start a new chat. Don't mix unrelated ideas in a single chat.
  • Don't correct the AI with a reply. Instead go back, edit your prompt, then re-run.
  • Remember that prompts, files, images etc all use up the context window.
  • Most important: the better the context you provide, the better the predictions (outputs).

Workflow Platform Comparison

Choosing the right automation platform depends on your technical skill, the complexity of your workflows, and your budget.

  • Zapier: Best for non-technical users and simple, linear workflows.
  • Make: Ideal for mid-technical users needing branching logic.
  • n8n: The top choice for developers and power users building bespoke, AI-rich flows.
